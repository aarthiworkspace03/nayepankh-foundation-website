About Us

NayePankh Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social change by empowering underprivileged individuals and communities. Guided by the belief that service to mankind is service to God, the foundation works towards building a more inclusive and compassionate society.

Our Initiatives

📚 Education

Providing learning opportunities and educational support to children from underserved communities.

👩 Women's Empowerment

Conducting skill-development and awareness programs to help women become financially and socially independent.

🤝 Community Relief

Supporting communities during emergencies and crises through relief drives and welfare programs.

Our Impact

🌟200,000+ lives impacted

🌟Active in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and other regions

🌟Hundreds of volunteers contributing to social change
