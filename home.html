NayePank Foundation




NayePankh Foundation aims to create positive social change by empowering underprivileged individuals and communities. Its core philosophy is that service to mankind is service to God, and it encourages volunteers to contribute their time, skills, and ideas to make a tangible impact in areas such as education, health, and crisis support.
